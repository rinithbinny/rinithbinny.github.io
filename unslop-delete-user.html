Account Deletion

Last updated: May 6, 2026

At Unslop, we believe you should have complete control over your personal data. If you no longer wish to use the app and want to permanently erase your profile and learning history, you can easily request an account deletion directly within the app.

How to Delete Your Account

To request that your account and associated data be deleted, please follow these simple steps inside the app:

  1. Open the Unslop app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab.
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the page.
  4. Tap the Delete Account button.
  5. When the confirmation prompt appears, select Yes.

Data Deletion & Retention Period

Immediate Deletion

When you confirm your account deletion request in the app, the process is initiated and completed immediately. There is no waiting or retention period.

Once you click "Yes", the following data types are permanently deleted from our servers:

  • Your User Row: Your entire profile, authentication details, and account settings.
  • Completed Topics: The history of all the topic summaries, books, and insights you have read.
  • Saved Ideas: Any ideas, concepts, or cards you have saved or bookmarked within the app.

Please note: This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your earned screen time, saved knowledge, and account data cannot be recovered.

Contact Support

If you have uninstalled the app and need assistance with deleting your account, or if you have any questions regarding how your data is handled, please contact us directly at: hello@mewguys.com. We will manually process your deletion request within 7 business days.
