SweatPass ‚Äì Screen time you have to earn

Instead of blocking your phone or nagging you, SweatPass uses movement as the reward system. Minutes go down while you scroll, and refill only when you work out.

How SweatPass works

SweatPass connects your most distracting apps to your workouts. You pick which apps to lock ‚Äî Instagram, YouTube, games, anything.

The app gives you a balance of Screen Time Minutes. When you use a locked app, those minutes count down. Hit zero, and SweatPass instantly locks those apps.

To unlock them again, you have to complete a workout inside SweatPass. The camera tracks your movement, counts reps automatically, and converts real effort into new minutes.

  • Time becomes a currency you earn, not something you lose control over.
  • Habits form naturally because workouts are tied to daily phone usage.
  • Ideal for students, professionals, and anyone battling screen addiction.
Workout engine
AI pose tracking (camera) No wearables needed

SweatPass has built-in, camera-tracked workouts. Just place your phone, follow the moves, and let the AI handle rep counting.
