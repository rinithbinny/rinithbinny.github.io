Turn Screen Time Into Workout Time

SweatPass is the revolutionary digital wellbeing and fitness app that **rewards activity** by linking access to your distracting apps directly to completing a workout. Earn your screen timeâ€”don't just restrict it.

How SweatPass Builds Discipline Through Action

It creates a positive habit loop: Your favorite apps become the **reward currency** you generate only by being active. This works better than traditional restrictive screen time methods.

Time as Currency

Choose distracting apps (Instagram, games, etc.). Using them depletes your daily Screen Time Minutes. Hit zero, and the apps lock instantly.

Workouts Generate Minutes

Unlock your apps instantly by completing a workout *inside* SweatPass. This flips the script: screen time is **earned**, not lost.

AI Pose Tracking

No equipment or wearable needed. SweatPass uses your phone's camera and AI to accurately track workouts like Push-ups, Squats, and Plank reps automatically.

System-Level Locking

We use robust, privacy-first system controls (like Android's Accessibility service) to enforce the app lock, ensuring the motivation loop is simple and effective.
