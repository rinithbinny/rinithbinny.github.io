Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Unslop (“we”, “our”, “the App”) collects, uses, and protects your information. Unslop is designed to help you build healthy digital habits by requiring you to read summarized knowledge and answer educational questions to earn screen time. We value your privacy and are committed to keeping your data safe.

By using Unslop, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Unslop collects only the minimum information required to provide its core learning and screen-time functionality, handle subscriptions, and serve ads.

1.1 App Usage Information

To determine when to lock or unlock selected apps, Unslop may collect:

  • The name and package ID of the app currently on screen.
  • The list of apps you choose to lock.

This information is processed locally on your device. Unslop does not collect or store the content of any app you use.

1.2 Learning and Assessment Data

To grant you screen time, Unslop asks you questions based on the topic or book summaries you read:

  • We collect your responses to multiple-choice questions (MCQs) and written text answers.
  • Written answers are securely sent to our AI integration (Google Gemini) to evaluate their accuracy and relevance.

1.3 In-App Subscriptions (Pro Pass)

Unslop offers an optional premium subscription called pro_pass, which unlocks features and provides an ad-free experience. Payment processing is securely handled entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your billing information.

1.4 Advertising Data (AdMob)

For users who do not have the pro_pass, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers and IP addresses to provide ads. You can manage your ad preferences through your device settings.

2. Google Gemini AI Usage

Unslop utilizes Google Gemini AI to review the short written answers you provide after reading a topic.

  • Data Sent to AI: Only the text of your written answer and the context of the question are sent to Google Gemini AI.
  • No Personal Information: We do not attach your personal identity, name, or device identifiers to the text sent to the AI.
  • Purpose: The AI is solely used to verify if your answer demonstrates a correct understanding of the topic so you can be awarded screen time.

3. Hardware Permissions

No Camera or Microphone Used:

Unlike some other productivity or fitness apps, Unslop does not request, require, or use your device camera or microphone. We do not collect any images, face data, video, or audio recordings of our users.

4. How We Use Your Information

Unslop uses your data only to:

  • Detect when a locked app is opened.
  • Apply app blocking based on your remaining earned time.
  • Evaluate your answers (via Gemini AI) to grant you screen time limits.
  • Verify your pro_pass subscription status.
  • Serve relevant advertisements (unless you have the ad-free Pro Pass).
Our Commitment:
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not share your reading habits or text answers with advertisers.
  • We do not collect biometrics or camera data.

5. Android Accessibility Service Disclosure

On Android, Unslop uses the Accessibility Service solely to detect when selected apps are opened and to display the Unslop blocking screen over them. This permission is strictly used to enforce the screen time limits you earn. This usage data is not stored permanently, transmitted to our servers, or shared with third parties.

6. iOS Screen Time API Disclosure

On iOS, Unslop uses the Apple Screen Time API to manage selected apps and enforce your earned usage limits securely. Unslop has no access to your personal content or activities inside those restricted apps.

7. Data Storage and Security

  • Unslop stores your local settings, earned time balance, and timer configurations on your device only.
  • Your written answers are processed ephemerally by our AI partner and are not logged or stored permanently on our servers.
  • If you uninstall the app, all local data is automatically deleted.
